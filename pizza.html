Pizza

The most popular Italian dish

Ingredients
  • 1kg Wheat Flour
  • 750ml Water
  • 5g Yeast
  • 20g Salt
  • 30ml Oil
Process
  1. Combine all the ingredients.
  2. Have fun kneading.
  3. Let it rest for a couple of hours.
  4. Turn on the oven to 'hell' temperature.
  5. Season your pizza as you want.
  6. Put it in the oven for 3 seconds.
  7. Buon Appetito!
